# QA Checklist

## Entry Flow

- Start from title and reach setup with one click.
- Verify participant counts 2, 4, and 8.
- Confirm names persist while changing car choices.
- Confirm every car can be selected without layout overflow.

## Race Flow

- Start at least five races and confirm different maps can appear.
- Confirm race duration resolves before 124 seconds.
- Confirm SP increases during visible drift moments.
- Confirm banana, rocket, turbo, shield, smoke, and line disruption appear in the event ticker across several races.
- Confirm the camera focuses on items, drifts, overtakes, and finishes.

## Results

- Confirm the last-ranked player is marked as coffee buyer.
- Confirm every player has rank, finish time, drift seconds, item count, and max speed.
- Confirm rematch works and setup return works.

## Browser Coverage

- Desktop: 1280 x 720 or larger.
- Mobile: 390 x 844 or similar.
- GitHub Pages: direct URL refresh should load the app.
